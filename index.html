About

Welcome to the captivating world of photography, where every click of the shutter unveils vivid moments frozen in time. With a passion for capturing life's beauty, I'm Zyron Manangan, a dedicated student at Pangasinan State University Bayambang - Campus, is adept at transforming ordinary scenes into extraordinary works of art. With an eye for detail and a creative spirit, with 5 years of experience in the art of photography, weaving together stunning visual narratives that invite viewers to experience unforgettable moments through his lens. Armed with a mastery of various photography techniques and a deep understanding of composition, I invite you to join on my journey through the vibrant landscapes and moving stories that await within his captivating portfolio. Let's bring your imagination into focus and create unforgettable images together.

Experience

As a professional photographer, I specialize in capturing stunning images and moments through my lens. I have extensive experience in both digital and film photography, and have worked on a wide range of projects including portraits, landscapes, and commercial work. I have a portfolio of over 500 images and have been recognized for my work in several photography competitions and exhibitions. In addition to my personal work, I also share photography tips and techniques on my Instagram account, where I have helped thousands of aspiring photographers improve their skills and pursue their passion for photography.
